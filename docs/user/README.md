## Getting Started

## Fleet Management

Manage your delivery fleet including couriers, vehicles, and assignments.

### Couriers

#### Adding a New Courier

1. Navigate to **Fleet → Couriers**
2. Click **"+ Add Courier"**
3. Fill in required information:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - National ID
   - Driver License Number
   - License Expiry Date
4. Upload required documents:
   - National ID (front and back)
   - Driver License
   - Photo
5. Click **"Create Courier"**

#### Managing Courier Information

**View Courier Details:**
1. Go to **Fleet → Couriers**
2. Click on courier name
3. View complete profile including:
   - Personal information
   - Documents
   - Assignment history
   - Performance metrics

**Edit Courier:**
1. Open courier profile
2. Click **"Edit"**
3. Update information
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

**Deactivate Courier:**
1. Open courier profile
2. Click **"Actions" → "Deactivate"**
3. Provide reason
4. Confirm deactivation

### Vehicles

#### Adding a New Vehicle

1. Navigate to **Fleet → Vehicles**
2. Click **"+ Add Vehicle"**
3. Enter vehicle details:
   - Plate Number
   - Brand & Model
   - Year
   - Color
   - Vehicle Type (Motorcycle, Car, Van, Truck)
   - Registration Expiry
   - Insurance Expiry
4. Upload documents:
   - Registration (Istimara)
   - Insurance
   - Photos
5. Click **"Create Vehicle"**

#### Vehicle Maintenance

**Schedule Maintenance:**
1. Go to **Fleet → Vehicles**
2. Click on vehicle
3. Go to **"Maintenance"** tab
4. Click **"+ Schedule Maintenance"**
5. Fill in details:
   - Maintenance Type (Oil Change, Inspection, Repair)
   - Scheduled Date
   - Service Provider
   - Estimated Cost
   - Notes
6. Click **"Schedule"**

**Record Maintenance:**
1. Open maintenance record
2. Click **"Mark as Completed"**
3. Enter actual details:
   - Completion Date
   - Actual Cost
   - Parts Replaced
   - Notes
4. Upload receipt
5. Click **"Save"**

### Assignments

#### Assigning Courier to Vehicle

1. Navigate to **Fleet → Assignments**
2. Click **"+ New Assignment"**
3. Select courier from dropdown
4. Select vehicle from dropdown
5. Set assignment dates:
   - Start Date
   - End Date (optional for permanent)
6. Add notes if needed
7. Click **"Create Assignment"**

#### Reassigning Vehicle

1. Go to **Fleet → Assignments**
2. Find active assignment
3. Click **"Reassign"**
4. Select new courier
5. Set new dates
6. Click **"Save"**

### Inspections

**Conduct Vehicle Inspection:**
1. Go to **Fleet → Inspections**
2. Click **"+ New Inspection"**
3. Select vehicle
4. Select inspector
5. Complete inspection checklist:
   - Exterior condition
   - Interior condition
   - Engine & mechanics
   - Tires & brakes
   - Lights & signals
   - Safety equipment
6. Add photos
7. Mark items as Pass/Fail
8. Add notes for failed items
9. Click **"Submit Inspection"**

## Operations

Manage deliveries, routes, COD, and incidents.

### Deliveries

#### Creating a Delivery

1. Navigate to **Operations → Deliveries**
2. Click **"+ New Delivery"**
3. Enter delivery details:
   - Tracking Number (auto-generated)
   - Customer Information
   - Pickup Address
   - Delivery Address
   - Scheduled Date & Time
   - COD Amount (if applicable)
   - Special Instructions
4. Assign to:
   - Courier (optional)
   - Vehicle (optional)
   - Route (optional)
5. Click **"Create Delivery"**

#### Tracking Deliveries

1. Go to **Operations → Deliveries**
2. Use filters:
   - Status (Pending, In Transit, Delivered, Failed)
   - Date Range
   - Courier
   - Zone
3. Click on delivery to view:
   - Current status
   - Location history
   - Status updates
   - Photos (if available)

#### Updating Delivery Status

1. Open delivery details
2. Click **"Update Status"**
3. Select new status:
   - Assigned
   - In Transit
   - Delivered
   - Failed
   - Cancelled
4. Add notes/photos
5. For delivery:
   - Capture signature
   - Take proof of delivery photo
   - Collect COD (if applicable)
6. Click **"Update"**

### Routes

**Create Route:**
1. Go to **Operations → Routes**
2. Click **"+ Create Route"**
3. Enter route details:
   - Route Name
   - Zone
   - Start Location
   - Deliveries to include
4. Optimize route (automatic)
5. Assign courier and vehicle
6. Click **"Create Route"**

**View Route Progress:**
1. Go to **Operations → Routes**
2. Click on active route
3. View:
   - Route map
   - Delivery sequence
   - Completed deliveries
   - Remaining deliveries
   - ETA

### COD Management

**Record COD Collection:**
1. When delivering, mark delivery as "Delivered"
2. Enter COD amount collected
3. System automatically records in COD log

**Deposit COD:**
1. Go to **Operations → COD**
2. View COD balance
3. Click **"Create Deposit"**
4. Enter:
   - Deposit Amount
   - Bank Details
   - Reference Number
5. Upload deposit slip
6. Click **"Submit Deposit"**

**View COD Reports:**
1. Go to **Operations → COD → Reports**
2. Select date range
3. View:
   - Total Collected
   - Total Deposited
   - Outstanding Balance
   - Courier-wise breakdown

### Incidents

**Report Incident:**
1. Navigate to **Operations → Incidents**
2. Click **"+ Report Incident"**
3. Select incident type:
   - Accident
   - Package Damage
   - Customer Complaint
   - Vehicle Breakdown
   - Other
4. Fill in details:
   - Date & Time
   - Location
   - Description
   - Related Delivery (if applicable)
5. Upload photos/documents
6. Click **"Submit Report"**

**Track Incident Resolution:**
1. Go to **Operations → Incidents**
2. Click on incident
3. View:
   - Status (Open, Under Investigation, Resolved, Closed)
   - Updates and comments
   - Resolution details

## FAQs

### General

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your email.

**Q: Can I use BARQ on mobile?**
A: Yes! Download the BARQ mobile app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).

**Q: How do I change my language?**
A: Go to Settings → Preferences → Language and select your preferred language.

### Fleet Management

**Q: How long does it take to approve a new courier?**
A: Courier approval typically takes 1-2 business days after all required documents are uploaded.

**Q: Can I assign one vehicle to multiple couriers?**
A: No, a vehicle can only be assigned to one courier at a time. You must end the current assignment before creating a new one.

**Q: When should I schedule vehicle maintenance?**
A: Schedule maintenance based on manufacturer recommendations, mileage, or when inspection identifies issues.

### Operations

**Q: Can I edit a delivery after it's created?**
A: Yes, but only certain fields can be edited depending on the delivery status. Completed deliveries cannot be edited.

**Q: How do I handle a failed delivery?**
A: Mark the delivery as "Failed", select the failure reason, add notes, and the system will automatically reschedule or create a follow-up task.

**Q: What happens if I collect incorrect COD amount?**
A: Contact your supervisor immediately. An incident report may need to be created, and adjustments will be made in the system.

### HR

**Q: How many annual leave days do I have?**
A: Check HR → Leave → My Leave Balance to see your available leave days.

**Q: How long does loan approval take?**
A: Loan requests are typically reviewed within 3-5 business days.

**Q: Can I clock in/out from my mobile?**
A: Yes, use the BARQ mobile app to clock in/out with GPS verification.

### Reports & Analytics

**Q: Can I export reports to Excel?**
A: Yes, most reports have an "Export to Excel" button. Some also support PDF and CSV exports.

**Q: How often are analytics updated?**
A: Real-time metrics update every 5 minutes. Daily aggregates update every hour. Historical data is updated nightly.
